The Best Movies According to Sonu

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Your Name

The anime "Your Name" (Kimi no Na wa) is a beautifully animated romantic fantasy film directed by Makoto Shinkai. It tells the story of two teenagers, Mitsuha Miyamizu and Taki Tachibana, who mysteriously begin to swap bodies intermittently.

Interstellar

Interstellar is a sci-fi epic directed by Christopher Nolan that explores themes of love, survival, and the complexities of space and time. Released in 2014, the film combines a gripping narrative with stunning visuals and a thought-provoking premise.
